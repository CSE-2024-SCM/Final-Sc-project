Welcome to Kannama Cooks

Kannama Cooks is a vibrant cloud kitchen dedicated to celebrating the rich culinary heritage of South India. From spicy Chettinad gravies to wholesome Ragi Mudde and soulful Andhra pappu, our menu offers a journey through Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh. We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients to prepare dishes that are not only mouthwateringly delicious but also healthy and hygienically cooked.
